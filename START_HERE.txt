## ✨ What JARVIS Can Do

### Voice Commands (Say These Out Loud)
- ✅ "What's the time?" → Shows current time
- ✅ "Tell me the date" → Shows today's date
- ✅ "Search for Python" → Opens Google search
- ✅ "Open YouTube" → Opens YouTube
- ✅ "Hello" → JARVIS responds
- ✅ "Tell me a joke" → JARVIS tells joke
- ✅ "Clear history" → Deletes all commands

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- ✅ **M** = Activate microphone
- ✅ **H** = Show history panel
- ✅ **C** = Clear history
- ✅ **Esc** = Close history
